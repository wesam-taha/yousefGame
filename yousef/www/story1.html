While reading the training, educators & teachers should:
Pronounce words carefully and correctly for the child to understand each word.
Adhere to silence at full stop, a number of seven full stops (......). at each end of a situation, the educator should stop and count to seven then continue to read, this is required to enable the child to think and continue the educator's sentence with the correct words.
Assist the child while performing the enclosed educational faith training activities while taking into consideration the related interaction of the child for trying to completely solve the overall questions & activities.
